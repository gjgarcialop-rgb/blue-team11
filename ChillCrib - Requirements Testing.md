# ChillCrib Requirements Testing
## Actors
- Customer
- Provider


### Use Cases

### 0. Customer/Provider: Create a proper homepage.
1. the customer and provider can access the homepage link, where they can 
choose whether to signin/signup as a provider/customer, or access the website as
a guest.

#### 1. Provider: Create/modify a provider account.
1. The signs up for the first time and creates an account. account creation 
depends on the correct information being filled out.
2. The provider can signout and sign back into their account. 
3. The provider can click their profile link to navigate to their profile.
4. Provider can edit details like Name, Email, Phone Number, and Address.

#### 2. Customer: Create/modify a customer account/profile:
1. Customer signs up for the first time and creates a an account.
2. The customer can signout and sign back in to their account. account creation 
depends on the correct information being filled out.
3. The customer can click their profile link to navigate to the profile page.
4. Customer can edit details like Name, Email, Phone Number, and Address.

#### 3. Customer: View and subscribe to ChillCrib Services
1. Customer can click on the subscriptions button or manage subscriptions button
to view available services.
2. Customer can click on a service and press save and continue to subscribe to 
the service. 
3. Customer can click remove button next to their current subscription to 
"cancel" the subscription.

### 4. Provider: Create/edit and view/delete Listings.
1. Provider clicks on Create a Crib link to navigate to the form.
2. Provider adds relevant information, and can upload images to the listing.
3. Provider successfully creates a listing after pressing Create Listing.
4. Provider can view all their listings by clicking on My Cribs Link.
5. Provider can edit listing by clicking the edit button.
6. The provider can delete a Listing by pressing the delete button.

### 5. Customer: View and Book a Crib, and view Bookings.
1. Customer clicks on View Cribs button to view available cribs.
2. Customer can click view details button to expand the listing.
3. Customer can click book now and add booking details.
4. Customer can checkout with a "credit card".
5. Customer can view Booking with My bookings Link.
6. Customer can cancel a booking with cancel button.

#### 6. Customer: Write a review
1. The Customer can navigate to Bookings and leave a review with Review button.
2. The customer can leave comments and a 0-5 star rating.
